Cicada-3032: The Cybersecurity Board Game Rules

Welcome to Cicada-3032

Cicada-3032 is a board game centered around cybersecurity in which players compete to be the first to reach the center of the board. To achieve this, you will use Bonus cards to protect and benefit yourself, and Penalty cards to slow down or block your opponents as they progress across the board.

Game Content

  • The game board
  • 4 pawns
  • 3 dice:
    • 1d10 (10-sided) – First Circle
    • 1d6 (6-sided) – Second Circle
    • 1d3 (3-sided) – Third Circle
  • 1 "Draw" deck of 64 cards:
    • 30 Bonus cards
    • 30 Penalty (Malus) cards
    • 4 Protection cards
  • 1 "Trap" deck of 32 cards (various effects)
  • 1 "Quiz" deck of 16 cards

Setting Up the Game

  • Place the Board: Unfold and place the board at the center of the table, with the decks next to it.
  • Choose Pawns: Each player chooses a pawn and places it on the Starting Space (note: this is not space 1).
  • Deal Cards: Deal 5 cards to each player.
  • Prepare Draw Deck: Add the 4 Protection cards to the "Draw" deck.
  • Shuffle: Shuffle each deck (Draw, Trap, Quiz).
  • Start: Begin the game!

Turns

A player's turn consists of 2 phases, which must be executed in order:

  • Draw Phase: The player draws one card from the Draw deck.
  • Action Phase: The player has 2 actions in a turn (1 Movement + 1 Card):
    • Movement: The player rolls the die and moves forward a number of spaces equal to the result. This includes any bonus/penalty effects from cards.
    • The player resolves the effect of the space they landed on.
    • Phase: The player may use one Bonus card or one Penalty card.

Types of Spaces

Symbol Space Type Effect
(Draw Symbol) Draw Space Draw one card from the Draw deck.
(Trap Symbol) Trap Space Draw one card from the Trap deck and apply its effect.
(+2) Move Forward Move forward 2 additional spaces.
(-2) Move Backward Move backward 2 spaces.
(?) Quiz Space (The effect for this space is not detailed in the rules provided.)

DMZ (Demilitarized Zone)

The DMZ is a safe zone where players cannot be targeted by card effects.

DMZ Rules:

  • Entering the DMZ: When a player enters the DMZ, they must immediately draw a Quiz card and answer it. If they answer correctly, they may move forward on their next turn; otherwise, they must answer another Quiz card on their next turn before they can advance.
  • Moving Back: If a player must move back several spaces and lands in or passes through the DMZ, they must still answer a Quiz card to be allowed to advance on a future turn.
  • Immunity: A player currently inside the DMZ cannot be targeted by other players' cards.

Cards

Types of Cards

Card Type Effect Deck
Bonus Grants a positive effect to the targeted player (usually the player using the card). Draw Deck
Penalty (Malus) Applies a negative effect to the targeted opponent. Draw Deck
Protection Grants protection against a specific card category. Draw Deck
Trap Applies a negative effect to one or more players. Trap Deck
Quiz A question that must be answered to progress (in the DMZ). Quiz Deck

Card Rules

Penalty (Malus) Cards

  • A player can only be affected by one card of a given type at a time. (Example: A player cannot be hit with two "Virus" cards simultaneously.)

Protection Cards

  • When a player uses a Protection card, they become immune to the associated card type.
  • Duration: The Protection card is available for the entire game.
  • Limit: A player can only have one Protection card.

Trap Cards

  • When a player lands on a Trap Space, they must draw a Trap card and apply its effect.
  • These cards apply a negative effect to one or more players.

End of the Game

The game ends when a player arrives at the Center Space. At this moment, the player must scan the QR code located behind the padlock using the QR Scan function in the add-on. The game leaves the surprise for you!
